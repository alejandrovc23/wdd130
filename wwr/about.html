Rapids! Make Memories!

Our goal is to provide you with the best experience possible. We have been providing unforgettable adventures for over 30 years. We are a family owned and operated business dedicated to safety, fun, and unforgettable moments with our team of professional guides.

History

Rapids! started floating the river in 1995. Since then, we have been providing thrilling and safe adventures for over 30 years. As a family-owned and operated business, our mission has always been to create lifelong memories for our customers. With experienced guides and a passion for nature, we are dedicated to delivering the best whitewater rafting experiences possible.
