White Water Rafting

At Rapids! we have been guiding adventurers for more than 30 years. Our mission is to share the thrill of white water rafting while keeping safety first. Whether you are a beginner or an expert paddler, our team of guides is ready to make your trip unforgettable.

History

Rapids! began exploring the river in 1995 with a small group of passionate rafters. Since then, we’ve grown into one of the most trusted rafting outfitters in the region, welcoming thousands of adventurers every summer.

Our family-owned business has always focused on personalized experiences. From scenic half-day floats to extreme Class V rapids, we continue to expand our trips while keeping the same commitment to safety and fun.
